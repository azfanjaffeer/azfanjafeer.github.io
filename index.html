The Silent Executive

How Bold Executives Effectively Contribute to Company Success.

About

The Silent Executive is a book dedicated to highlighting the behaviors and mindsets that successful (silent) executives use to effectively lead to their businesses. Drawing information from over 100 interviews with Senior Partners, Founders, C-Suites, and executives, The Silent Executive combines their knowledge and experience with the best practices of all business types from start-ups, to large enterprises.

With this book learn how Silent Executives are:

  • Clearly conveying values & vision
  • Leading teams effectively
  • Leveraging technology as business tools
  • Keeping design at the core
  • Building loyalty & trust with customers
  • Developing goal-oriented mindsets
  • Maximizing productivity
  • Fostering innovative environments
  • Autonomizing employees
  • Successfully working with consultants

About the author

Azfan Jaffeer has worked with clients from different industries, ranging from the hottest tech start-ups to international aerospace powerhouses. He holds a bachelors in Engineering from McGill University, and currently resides in Montreal, Canada. Jaffeer has worked as part of teams in engineering, sales and services. Currently, he is an enterprise consultant at Traction On Demand, a Canadian company specializing in the Salesforce.com platform. He focuses on helping clients identify the root causes of their issues, recommending industry best practices, and working with his team to build first class solutions. Jaffeer grew up in England, Australia, China, Vietnam, Austria and Sri Lanka and thrives in multi-cultural social settings.
